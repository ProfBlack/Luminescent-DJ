This is a compilation of event flag and constant changes. The idea is to take saves at various points in the story and see what flags and constants change.

So for example, the first save was made just before walking into lake verity, so the flags listed are the ones that have changed since the start of the game. The second save was made just before the catching tutorial and the flags shown are the ones that changed since the pre-lake-verity save. This is repeated until defeating the champion. 

The check points aren't very granular, they're just to give you an overview of what flags change during a given period of the story. There's alwasy a save directly before and after every gym.

Some caveats:
 - I updated my game from 1.1.0 to 1.1.1 after defeating team galactic in eterna. I should have done this from the beginning, but I didn't and I didn't want to go back and redo the earlier saves.
 - I tried to avoid trainers and items, but messed a couple of times, so some of the flags will denote trainers being defeated and items being collected.
 - I gave myself Arceus to get through the game quicker, I don't know if this affects any flags, but there's an outside chance there's an 'Arceus Acquired' flag.
